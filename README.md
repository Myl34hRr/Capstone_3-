# Capstone_3

Sales Territory Analysis Capstone
Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales performance across the South Region, including Florida, South Carolina, and Texas. The objective is to identify sales trends, top-performing stores, customer purchasing behavior, and product category performance to support data-driven business decisions and marketing strategies.

The analysis was completed using Python, Pandas, Excel datasets, and Power BI.


# Business Questions

1. Who are the territory managers assigned to the South Region?
2. What are the store IDs and locations for stores in Florida, South Carolina, and Texas?
3. What is the monthly total revenue generated in the South Region?
4. Which stores are the top-performing stores in the region?
5. Who are the top customers based on total spending?
6. What is the number of transactions per month by product category?
7. What is the total sales revenue per month by product category?
8. What recommendations can be made for future marketing efforts?
